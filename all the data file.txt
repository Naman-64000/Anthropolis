List of all simulation variables mapped to macro/demographic data (India 2024-2026):

=========================================
1. CITIZEN VARIABLES (Micro-Level Attributes)
=========================================
- citizen_id (int): Total population target (1,467,231,210 people equivalent)
- age (float): Median age baseline (28.7 years)
- baseline_health (float): Life expectancy at birth (70.8 years)
- health (float): Current physical health status (0.0 to 70.8)
- energy (float): Daily caloric intake (2,458 kcal capacity)
- education_level (float): Mean years of schooling (~6.7 years, maps to 0.45 index)
- bank_balance / net_worth (float): Wealth per adult ($1,712 median savings starting)
- debt (float): Household debt ($1,068 average per capita)
- sex (str): Sex ratio (1,020 Females per 1,000 Males overall; 50.5% M / 49.5% F starting)
- religious_affiliation (int): Stochastic cultural affiliation (Hindu: 79.8%, Muslim: 14.2%, Christian: 2.3%, Sikh: 1.7%, Other: 2.0%)
- religiosity (float): Importance of religion in decision-making (beta mean of 0.97)
- stress_level (float): Daily stress index (~35.0% average baseline)
- daily_earnings (float): Daily income generated ($3.5 to $8.5 minimum wage floor equivalent)
- last_monthly_income (float): Record of last monthly wage earned ($70 to $170 depending on education/skill)

=========================================
2. ENVIRONMENT NODE VARIABLES (Structural & Economic Entities)
=========================================
- node_id / node_name (str): Registered business entity identifier (~2.66M active registered companies)
- node_type (str): Business type (Workplace, Hospital, School, GroceryStore, Restaurant)
- capacity (int): Service capacity scaled (e.g. 50-80 slots)
- price (float): Consumer Price Index (CPI) baseline. Monthly inflation (4.75% annually)
- employee_capacity (int): Worker slots (Services: 34%, Industry: 22%, Agriculture: 44%)
- capital / base_capital (float): Stock capitalization ($93,700 base starting capital)
- occupied_slots (int): Active patient/student/customer strain count
- hospital_strain (float): Hospital bed occupancy rate (~65% peak baseline)

=========================================
3. SIMULATION ENGINE VARIABLES (Global State)
=========================================
- population_size (int): Base Population Target (1,467,231,210 individuals)
- initial_gov_capital (float): Sovereign Forex Reserves + Union Budget ($1.27 Trillion USD)
- pop_scale (float): Digital twin agent-to-people scale factor (1 Agent ≈ 9.78M People)
- government_capital (float): Scaled government treasury ($1.27 Trillion / pop_scale ≈ $129,836.37)
- dead_citizens (list): Cumulative deaths logged (~11,590,000 per year equivalent)
- total_wealth_inherited (float): Cumulative positive estate wealth transferred to heirs (~$14.2 Trillion proxy)
- total_wealth_escheated (float): Unclaimed assets seized by government (~$9.3 Billion proxy)
- dependency_ratio (float): Age dependency ratio ([0-14 and 60+] / [15-59] = ~47.5%)
- gini_coefficient (float): Wealth inequality coefficient (0.402 target baseline)
- unemployment_rate (float): Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS) unemployment rate (3.2%)
- seir_infected (int): TB/Endemic disease incidence cases (2.8 million active cases baseline)
- private_capital (float): Total private corporate capital (~$14.2 Trillion proxy)

=========================================
4. GOVERNMENT POLICIES & PARAMETERS
=========================================
- tax_rate (float): Starting personal income tax rate (0.05 / 5% default slab)
- corporate_tax_rate (float): Base corporate tax rate (0.22 / 22% default)
- interest_rate (float): Weighted Average Lending Rate (0.098 / 9.8% WALR default)
- fast_food_tax (float): GST on restaurant food (0.05 / 5%)
- grocery_subsidy (float): Public Distribution System / PMGKAY (-0.80 / 80% subsidy)
- healthcare_subsidy (float): PM-JAY Ayushman Bharat subsidy (0.40 / 40% subsidy)
- education_subsidy (float): Free schooling tuition subsidy (1.0 / 100% free RTE)
- free_emergency_care (bool): Legal mandate for emergency medical care (True)
- ubi_amount (float): Universal Basic Income DBT transfers ($0.0 default)

=========================================
5. TRACKED MACRO & HISTORICAL METRICS
=========================================
- history_df (dataframe): NSO macro database containing time-series runs
- gini_coefficient / unemployment_rate / dependency_ratio: Mapped from simulation metrics
- private_capital / government_capital: Economic footprint aggregates
- seir_susceptible / seir_exposed / seir_infected / seir_recovered: SEIR progression tracking

=========================================
6. COMMAND-LINE CONFIGURATIONS & DEFAULTS
=========================================
- --pop / population_size (int): Base Population Target (1,467,231,210 individuals)
- --gov / initial_gov_capital (float): Initial Sovereign Corpus ($1.27 Trillion USD)
- --seed / seed (int): Base Year / Census Benchmark (2024 starting fiscal year)
- --ticks / ticks (int): NITI Aayog Planning Horizon (180 months / 15 years)
- --output / output (str): MoSPI National Accounts data repository ("mospi_national_accounts_2024_2039.csv")

=========================================
7. DASHBOARD UI STATE & TRANSIENT VARIABLES
=========================================
- pop_size_cr (float slider): Population scale range (130.0 to 160.0 Crores, default 146.72 Crores)
- gov_cap_b (float slider): Sovereign treasury range ($100.0B to $5000.0B, default $1270.0B)
- seed_val (int input): Seed default 2024
- ticks_to_run (int slider): Plan horizon range (12 to 360, default 180)
- dead_count (int): Cumulative deaths (~11,590,000 per year)
- infected_count (int): Active TB cases (~2.8 Million)
- diff_pop (int): Annual population growth (~11,737,849 / 0.8% growth)
- diff_gini (float): Gini coefficient volatility (-0.001 to +0.002)
- diff_unemp (float): Seasonal unemployment rate volatility (±0.2%)
- diff_gov (float): Fiscal deficit target (-4.9% of GDP)
- diff_infect (int): Year-over-year disease delta (-2% TB incidence decay)
- males / females (list): NFHS-5 gender split (Males 50.5%, Females 49.5%)
- born_males / born_females (int): Annual births (Males: ~11.92M, Females: ~11.08M)
- bins / labels (list): Demographic pyramid buckets ([0-14: 25.3%], [15-59: 64.6%], [60+: 10.1%])
- male_counts / female_counts (list): Bulk bulge age-pyramid counts (concentrated in 15-35 dividend cohorts)
- node_names / node_capitals / node_employees / node_capacities: Services (53% of GDP, 34% employees), Industry (28% of GDP, 22% employees), Agriculture (18% of GDP, 44% employees)

=========================================
8. INTERNAL MATHEMATICAL CONSTANTS & FUNCTIONAL INTERMEDIATE VARIABLES
=========================================
- alpha, beta, lambda_ (float): Prospect theory coefficients (alpha=0.88, beta=0.88, lambda_=2.25, high religiosity debt lambda = 9.0)
- A_s, B_s, c_val (float): Gompertz-Makeham mortality parameters (Background risk A=0.002, Aging B=0.00004, Exp rate c=1.095 for Male)
- energy_factor / poverty_factor (float): Multidimensional poverty risk multiplier (~1.75x mortality risk spike)
- k, c (float): Biomedical recovery rates reflecting hospital Average Length of Stay (ALOS) of 4.5 days
- tuition (float): Private school fee ($3.25 node price * 10 = $32.5/month)
- p_grocery (float): Monthly food inflation basket cost per capita ($1.50 node price * 20 = $30.0/month)
- p_fast / fee (float): Out-of-pocket healthcare admission fee ($133.33 node price * 3 = $400.0)
- interest / min_payment / payment (float): Debt repayment equated monthly installment (37.5% EMI-to-income ratio)
- starvation_loss_penalty (float): Prospect theory food utility penalty (-100.0)
- u_grocery / u_fast_food / u_starve (float): Consumer choices model utility weights (groceries weighted 1.5x, fast food 0.8x)
- share (float): Estate inheritance distribution (1/N equal offspring split)
- asfr_base / asfr_actual (float): Age-specific fertility rate (Peak 20-24: 122.9/1000 women/year, Peak 25-29: 112.5/1000 women/year)
- rel_diff / aff_diff / wealth_diff / similarity (float): Assortative mating homophily (90%+ intra-religion marriage coefficient constraint)
- effective_tax_rate (float): Personal income tax rate yield (approx. 6.6% of GDP total yield)
- A, K, L (float): Cobb-Douglas inputs (Capital share alpha=0.42, Labor share beta=0.58, TFP growth factor 2.2% annually)
- market_fluctuation (float): Economic volatility based on India VIX (~14.5% annual std dev)
- workplace_infectious_beta (float): Workplace epidemiological R0 beta transmission (0.35 in dense workplaces vs 0.20 open nodes)

=========================================
9. SCENARIO ANALYSIS & CALIBRATION TARGETS
=========================================
- target_fertility_rate: Total Fertility Rate (TFR) target of 2.09 births per woman
- target_crude_death_rate: Crude Death Rate target of 7.9 deaths per 1000 per year
- target_median_age: Median age target of 28.7 years
- target_workplace_infectious_rate: Localized workplace transmission multiplier of 1.75x
